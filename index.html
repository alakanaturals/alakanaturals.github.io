Our Story

Powerful by Tradition.

The Collection

Three herbs. Endless ritual.

Used alone or layered together, our trio covers conditioning, colour and strength — the complete natural routine.

Lawsonia inermis

Henna Powder

Nourish · Strengthen · Repair

A rich reddish-brown tint that conditions as it colours. Henna coats each strand, smooths the cuticle and leaves hair glossy and stronger — with zero chemical dyes.

  • Natural reddish-brown colour
  • Conditions scalp & strands
  • Strengthens and adds shine

Indigofera tinctoria

Indigo Powder

Deep · Natural · Black

Applied after henna, indigo transforms warm tones into deep browns and blacks. A natural, two-step way to cover greys — no PPD, no harsh oxidants.

  • Natural black & brown tones
  • Covers grey gracefully
  • Pairs with henna for depth

Emblica officinalis

Amla Powder

Root · Shine · Vitality

The Indian gooseberry, rich in natural Vitamin C. Amla strengthens roots, deepens henna colour and helps slow premature greying while adding mirror-like shine.

  • Strengthens roots & growth
  • Reduces premature greying
  • Enhances henna & adds shine

The Ritual

Four simple steps.

A slow, sensory routine — the way herbal care is meant to be.

  1. 01

    Mix

    Blend the powder with water into a smooth, lump-free paste. Let it rest a few minutes.

  2. 02

    Apply

    Section clean, damp hair and apply evenly from root to tip with a brush or gloved hands.

  3. 03

    Rest

    Cover and leave for 1–2 hours. The longer it sits, the deeper and richer the result.

  4. 04

    Rinse

    Rinse thoroughly with water. Skip shampoo for 24 hours to let the colour settle and deepen.

For external use only · Patch test recommended · Store cool & dry, away from moisture and sunlight.
